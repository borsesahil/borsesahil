Design Report

The design of the Department of E-Transport website is crafted to achieve a user-friendly, visually appealing, and accessible platform to effectively communicate our goals and engage our audience.

Design Decisions:

We opted for a clean and minimalistic design that prioritizes content and ease of navigation. The consistent use of our department logo and color scheme ensures a cohesive brand identity across all pages.

Web Standards:

We adhere to HTML5/XHTML5 markup to ensure compatibility with modern web browsers. This promotes semantic structure, accessibility, and improved SEO performance.

Accessibility Standards:

We are committed to accessibility. All images have descriptive alt tags to accommodate screen readers. Our color choices consider potential visual impairments, and text sizes are adjustable to ensure legibility for all users.

Responsiveness:

Our design is responsive and adapts seamlessly to various devices, from desktops to mobile phones. The use of CSS media queries allows content to be presented optimally on different screen sizes, ensuring an enjoyable experience for all users.

Benefits of Adopting Our Design:

  • Improved user experience and engagement through easy navigation and clean presentation.
  • Enhanced accessibility, ensuring inclusivity for all visitors.
  • Compliance with web and accessibility standards, resulting in better search engine visibility.
  • Mobile responsiveness reaches a wider audience and accommodates on-the-go users.
  • Consistent branding enhances the department's professional image.
