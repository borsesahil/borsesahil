About Us

The Department of E-Transport is dedicated to driving the future of transportation through sustainable and innovative solutions. Since its establishment in 2023, our department has been a key player in promoting eco-friendly and efficient e-transportation options.

Headquartered in the heart of the city, our department is located at:

26 Arnold Street
Holland Park,
Brisbane - 4006

Our primary goal is to assist e-transport in all its forms, whether it's electric vehicles, smart infrastructure, or alternative energy sources. By fostering partnerships, conducting research, and advocating for change, we aim to make e-transport a successful and sustainable solution for the modern world.
